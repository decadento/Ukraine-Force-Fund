To begin with, about us...

We are a young, progressive non-profit foundation with a small, active and fast-paced audience, which aims to support the Armed Forces of Ukraine with everything they need... From weapons and cars to personal hygiene items.

What do we do...

We have only one goal - to help the Armed Forces in every possible way. In today's conditions, the military needs a lot of tools, ammunition and means of transportation. All funds collected are spent exclusively on the most necessary things, which is regularly reported by our team.

It is time...

When is it more important than now to help our defenders. To make it easier and safer, we have created our resource, where you can be sure that your contribution will be applied 100%. To save the life of the heroes, you can support them with the help of our fund.

Frequently asked questions:

How to apply for military assistance? On our website you can find a link to a form to fill out. There, after specifying the data, your application will be sent to us. After processing, you will receive a confirmation email.

How long to wait for help? We consider all applications, but first of all we give preference to military personnel who are directly on the front line, participating in operations, etc. The estimated time from processing the application to the transfer of assistance is less than a month, but it depends on the amount of the fee.

What does the fund collect for? We have a wide range of assistance to the Armed Forces. We try to provide the fighters with everything they need. Sometimes when meetings close with surplus funds, they are spent on small but important items such as winter uniform kits, tourniquets and tactical headphones.
